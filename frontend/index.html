About Us

GP Soldiers Academy is dedicated to transforming young aspirants into proud soldiers of the Indian Army.

Our Mission

To provide disciplined, structured and result-oriented training that builds not just knowledgeable candidates, but confident, physically fit, and mentally strong future soldiers ready to serve the nation.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most trusted and successful defence academy in North Karnataka, consistently producing top-ranking selections in the Indian Army year after year.

What Makes Us Different

  • Ex-Army personnel and experienced faculty as trainers
  • 95%+ selection rate in recent batches
  • Complete preparation: Written + Physical + Medical + Interview
  • Daily physical training with professional equipment
  • Regular mock tests based on latest exam patterns
  • Comprehensive study material + previous year question banks
  • Hostel & mess facility with disciplined environment
  • Personal guidance and motivational sessions
